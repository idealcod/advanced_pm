# Capstone Project Topic: Ramadan ARC

## 1. Project Title

Ramadan ARC

## 2. Topic Area

AI Systems

## 3. Problem Statement

Ramadan is the holiest month for Muslims, but many Muslims may spend it with less benefit than they hoped. Some users may be negligent about the month, miss good deeds, or not fully understand the importance and opportunities of Ramadan.

The main problems are:

- Important worship times can be missed, including suhur, iftar, and prayer times.
- Users may forget daily dhikr, duas, and good deeds.
- Users may not have a structured way to build consistent spiritual habits.
- Users may need accessible Islamic reminders and educational content.
- Users may want help learning Arabic to better connect with Islamic texts.

## 4. Proposed Solution

Ramadan ARC is a digital assistant that helps Muslims spend Ramadan with maximum benefit. It supports users by reminding them about important worship times, encouraging good deeds, providing duas and hadiths, and helping them develop iman through consistent habits.

The system also includes a planned AI-assisted Arabic learning feature, allowing users to practice and learn Arabic in a more personalized way.

## 5. Target Users

Muslims who want to improve their Ramadan routine, remember key worship activities, and develop stronger spiritual habits.

## 6. Technology Stack

- Frontend: Next.js
- Backend: Golang
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI: Arabic language lesson assistant

## 7. Key Features

- Alarm clock before suhur with strong wake-up behavior.
- Alarm clock for iftar time.
- List of possible duas.
- Dhikr reminders.
- Prayer time reminders.
- Arabic language lessons with AI.
- List of hadiths.
- Habit tracking for good deeds.
- Analytics for completed habits and user activity.
